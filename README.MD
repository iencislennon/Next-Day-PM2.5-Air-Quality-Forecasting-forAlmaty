Business Problem: Predict next-day mean PM2.5 so the city can pre-issue health dvisories and trigger traffic/heating mitigations.
Why it matters: On Jan 14, 2025 Almaty ranked #1 of 125 cities on IQAir's real-time
global pollution ranking (AQI+ 256, "very unhealthy"), ahead of Cairo and Delhi; its 2024
annual mean PM2.5 was 20.3 µg/m³ — roughly 4× the WHO guideline of 5 µg/m³.
Vehicle emissions account for ~60% ofAlmaty's air pollution, with coal/biomass home
heating the main winter driver — a clear, high-stakes public-health forecasting problem.
